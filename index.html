Ashutosh met Jasaswini under a sky that forgot how to rain. Since then, even silence sounded like her name.

Our Story

It all began with a random phone call at 11:30 PM on April 29th. Jasaswini answered, and for the next half hour, we spoke like old souls meeting again after lifetimes apart. She was still healing from her past, unsure and guarded, but I didn’t ask for her heart—I just listened, stayed, and slowly became her peace. Somewhere between laughter, late-night talks, and quiet pauses, I fell in love with that beautiful chaos she carried so gracefully. When I first told her how I felt, she said no—and it felt like the longest silence I’d ever heard. But just two days later, she said yes—and in that moment, everything in me knew it was real. For the next three years, we lived a love that was raw, loud, soft, and everything in between. We shared food, fights, dreams, and dozens of "us" moments that still echo in the quiet. But love isn’t always enough when life keeps testing your edges. Misunderstandings grew, priorities shifted, and slowly, without wanting to, we began to drift. In the end, we chose peace over pain—even if it meant letting go. Still, no matter where life takes me, she’ll always be the most beautiful chapter my heart ever got to write.
